10 / QUESTIONS

The details,
made clear.

Still curious? Talk to our team ↗

What is an AI clone?+

An AI clone is an authorized digital identity trained on creator-approved knowledge, preferences and communication patterns to support audience interactions.

How is the AI trained?+

You provide selected content, FAQs, voice samples if desired, and brand rules. Your clone is shaped through a guided training and review process.

Can I control what it says?+

Yes. Define subjects, offers, tone, boundaries, prohibited topics and when a conversation must be handed to a human.

Will my audience know they are speaking with AI?+

The platform supports clear disclosure options so interactions can be transparent and appropriate for your audience.

Is voice cloning optional?+

Absolutely. Voice is opt-in, requires explicit authorization, and can be disabled independently.

Can agencies manage multiple creators?+

Yes. Agency workspaces support multiple profiles, permissions, shared reporting and team workflows.

How long does setup take?+

Timing depends on the content and experiences you want to support. Early-access creators receive guided onboarding.
